MetaLads is a collection of 10,001 randomized and uniquely built 3D Lads with the intention of opening our genesis assets to the public domain.
We have so many projects planned out for the future of MetaLads' ecosystem such as our own marketplace, tapping our brand into the mainstream, and more!

ROADMAP

Q1

Public Domain

We plan to release all our assets for the Genesis MetaLads collection under the Creative Commons Public Domain License to let the community take full advantage of our Lads, entirely indepdenent from our brand.

Q2

Marketplace

A marketplace will soon be in the works. We want to provide a way for MetaLad owners to fully pimp out their Lads and themselves with accessories from all ends of the MetaVerse, but definitely from within our future collections.

Q3

Collaborations + Mainstream

We are looking towards partnering with some big brands to bring their visions and assets to the MetaLads ecosystem with the hope of expanding our reach to mainstream media.

Q4

Open World

A huge dream would be briding MetaLads to the rest of the world. We are exploring ways to provide tools to integrate MetaLads into the Metaverse for developers and creators alike.
